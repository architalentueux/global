ANNONACEAE GLOBAL PHYLOGENTICS GROUP

Members of the Annonaceae Global Phylogenetics Consortium

The AGPC aims to generate a global phylogeny of all Annonaceae species. The initial step of this project started with the ERC GLOBAL project, which sequenced 85% of all species at the time.

Below, list of all members of AGPC in alphabetical order that contributed data, analyses or expertise and are co-authors of subsequent articles or oral communications.
